One step of many in my quest for pack rat reform

Hello! I have collected a lot of cool and weird stuff over the past forever and it's time to let some of it go. Instead of donating or selling it explicitly I'm doing this instead: sending it to you.

So here's how this will work. You give me your address + the maximum amount of shipping you're willing to pay, and I'll send you some stuff. Items may include:

  • books
  • stickers
  • posters and prints
  • maps
  • t-shirts
  • art supplies
  • home goods
  • uncategorizable nonsense

To send one of these packages will cost somewhere in the range of $8 to $18 depending on what's in each (based on USPS rates for media mail and flat rate packages). Once I put together your package, I'll send you an email with the exact amount it will cost to ship (no more than your maximum) and we can arrange a payment situation.

I recognize that it's a weird and hard time for a lot of people, and I'd love to bring some bits of joy to your life through random items I've acquired regardless of funds. Please feel free to put 0 in the form below and I can definitely send you something.

Thank you for doing me this favor! You're really helping me out and I appreciate every one of you.
